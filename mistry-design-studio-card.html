Our Process
Services
01
Block Selection Advice

Expert guidance to select a block with the best development potential — considering planning controls, orientation, access and feasibility.

02
Concept Design & 3D Massing

Tailored concept design plans with 3D massing studies to clearly visualise the proposal and build client confidence from day one.

03
Detailed Design

Further develop the approved concept into a detailed design, exploring variations in response to feedback, authority requirements and budget.

04
Façade Design

Develop the façade to complement the street character and architectural style. Two to three options prepared in collaboration with you.

05
Approvals & Construction Drawings

Comprehensive drawings coordinated for submission to certifiers, authorities and consultants to obtain all required approvals.

06
Site Assistance

Ongoing support during construction to address queries, coordinate clarifications and manage design variations as needed.
